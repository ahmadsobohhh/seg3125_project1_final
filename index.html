Our Glorious History!

Our History

uODentists is a well-known name in dental and oral care in Ottawa. The journey of this institution started in 17 B.C. under the hands of Dr. Ahmad Soboh, Valedictorian of uOttawa. uODentists dental centre has been leading the way in dental treatment for uOttawa students for more than 2,000 years in keeping with the evolution of time and the modernization of the era.
